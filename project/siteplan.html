A website plan for TERRAEssencias

Site Name

TERRAEssencias essential oils and natural products

Site Purpose

Providing a site for customers to find valuable information about the copany products like therapeutical properties, characteristics, how to use them, and how they are produced. The site will also provide with means to reach customer assistance. It will provide with landing pages for social media advetisement and a segment of customer journey composed by consideration and conversion stages. It will offer education content for customer to learn about reserch and science founds related to our products

Scenarios

What is the composition of lavanda essential oil and its therapeutical properties?

How should I use the product

What are all the products of your company?

Where can I find more info. about the company?
